English

How to Request Data Deletion:

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your data associated with the "Xe ve" application.

To request the deletion of your data that we have collected via Facebook Login, please send an email to:

thanhtuan2494@gmail.com

Please use the following subject line for your email: "Request for Data Deletion - Xe ve".

In the body of your email, please include your name and the email address associated with your Facebook account so we can accurately identify and delete your data.

We will process your request and delete your data from our systems within 30 days of receiving your request.
